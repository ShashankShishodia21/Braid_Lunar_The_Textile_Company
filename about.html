About Us

"TEXTILE OF BHARAT"

Braid Lunar is a cutting-edge textile company that seamlessly intertwines tradition with innovation, creating a tapestry of excellence in the world of fabrics. With a commitment to quality craftsmanship and a passion for pushing the boundaries of textile technology, Braid Lunar weaves together a rich legacy of expertise with a forward-thinking approach. Specializing in the production of high-performance and sustainable textiles, the company takes pride in offering a diverse range of fabrics that cater to the evolving needs of modern consumers and industries. Braid Lunar's dedication to ethical practices, coupled with a keen eye for design trends, positions it as a leading force in the textile industry, providing clients with not just fabrics, but an experience that reflects the harmony between artistry and functionality. Whether it's fashion, home decor, or industrial applications, Braid Lunar sets the standard for textiles that inspire, innovate, and elevate.
